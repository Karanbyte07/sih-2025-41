Advanced Marine Intelligence Platform

Cutting-edge AI technologies and comprehensive data processing capabilities designed specifically for marine research and conservation

AI-Powered Otolith Analysis

Revolutionary computer vision models analyze fish otolith morphology with 95% accuracy. Automated species identification, age determination, and growth pattern analysis for 50+ marine species.

Environmental DNA Intelligence

Advanced genomic sequencing pipeline processes 1K+ eDNA samples to detect marine biodiversity. Non-invasive species monitoring with 99% reliability across diverse aquatic ecosystems, identifying 50+ marine species.

Cloud-Native Architecture

Scalable microservices infrastructure built on Docker and PostgreSQL. Real-time data processing with RabbitMQ queues handling 1K+ sample datasets seamlessly across 15+ partner institutions.

Join the revolution in ocean science. Our platform empowers researchers, conservationists, and marine biologists with cutting-edge AI tools for unprecedented insights into marine ecosystems and biodiversity patterns.
